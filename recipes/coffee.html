French Press Coffee



Description

We'll talk about how to brew a simple cup of coffee in a french press-
a classic you can enjoy every day.


Ingredients

  • 5 tablespoons of ground coffee
  • 12 oz water
  • Milk (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Grind coffee beans (or use pre ground coffee) and place
    5 tablespoons in a French press.
  2. Heat 12 oz water until boiling, then place in French press,
    pushing plunger down to the top of the water.
  3. Let brew for 5 minutes.
  4. Pour coffee in a cup.
  5. Add sugar and cream if desired.
